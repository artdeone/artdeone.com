2. Title > Pronunciation and Meaning of ART de ONE
The name ART de ONE is inspired by a combination of languages and principles of design. The term "de" came in French and translates to "of" in English.
So, ART de ONE could be defined as "Art of One," emphasizing uniqueness, independence, and togetherness in artistic.

English pronunciation: "Art de one" is pronounced as
/ɑːrt də wʌn/ (Art Duh One)

Burmese pronunciation: To sound more natural in Burmese, we pronounce it as
အာ့ ဒ် ဝမ်း (Art D'Wan)

Title : The Story Behind
        the ART de ONE Logo

        The ART de ONE logo was inspired by the iconic clock of the Musée d'Orsay, which represents both art and precision.  I studied its curves and proportions, then used golden ratio circles to replicate its timeless harmony.  By overlaying the circular shapes and aligning them with a polar grid, the design started to show up.  The final symbol finds a balance between uniqueness and organization perfectly capturing the core values of ART de ONE.
