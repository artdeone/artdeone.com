Free Course: Photoshop Basics for Beginners

Your journey into the world of digital art starts here. This course covers the essential tools and techniques to get you started with Adobe Photoshop.

About This Course

Welcome to our introductory course on Adobe Photoshop! Whether you're a complete beginner or just need a refresher, this course is designed to be easy to follow. We'll guide you through the Photoshop interface, show you how to work with layers, make selections, and use the most fundamental tools for photo editing and graphic creation. By the end, you'll have the confidence to start your own projects.

What You'll Learn

  • Navigate the Photoshop workspace and understand its core components.
  • Master the concept of layers, the foundation of non-destructive editing.
  • Use basic selection tools like the Marquee, Lasso, and Magic Wand.
  • Perform simple retouching and color adjustments on photos.
  • Save and export your projects for web and print.
