Free Course: Photoshop Basics for Beginners

Your journey into the world of digital art starts here. This course covers the essential tools and techniques to get you started with Adobe Photoshop.

About This Course

Welcome to our introductory course on Adobe Photoshop! We'll guide you through the interface, how to work with layers, make selections, and use the most fundamental tools for photo editing and graphic creation.

What You'll Learn

  • Navigate the Photoshop workspace.
  • Master the concept of layers.
  • Use basic selection tools like Marquee and Lasso.
  • Perform simple retouching and color adjustments.
  • Save and export your projects.

Free Course: Quick Designs with Canva
