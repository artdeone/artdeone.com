The Concept

I used Adobe Illustrator to capture Venom's raw energy. The design blends organic textures with sharp, aggressive forms to show just how chaotic the character is.

Instead of using gradients or mesh tools, I used complex shapes and a simple color palette to create depth. This is a very hard look to achieve. The lighting gives the suit a shiny look without losing that intense, scary vibe.
