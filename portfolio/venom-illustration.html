The Concept

This digital illustration explores the duality of the Venom character, merging organic textures with sharp, aggressive symbiotic forms. The goal was to capture the raw energy and chaotic nature of the subject using Adobe Illustrator's vector capabilities.

By utilizing complex gradients and mesh tools, we achieved a sense of depth and realism that is often difficult to produce in vector art. The high contrast lighting emphasizes the glossy texture of the suit while maintaining the menacing atmosphere of the character.

Project Details

  • Tools Used: Adobe Illustrator, Wacom Tablet
  • Category: Character Design / Vector Art
  • Client: Personal Project
