The Concept

The "THE BURMA FOOD HOUSE" logo captures a modern Myanmar vibe while preserving traditional culture. We integrated iconic elements like a dumpling and chef's hat to immediately communicate the authentic culinary experience and professional service.

Design Approach

The most challenging and unique feature is the Custom Typography. We crafted English letters to mimic the rounded, looping aesthetic of Burmese script.
