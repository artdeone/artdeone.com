Brand Identity Project

ONE DAY STUDIO

A minimal and modern identity design representing creative excellence.

Back to Portfolio

The Concept

The "One Day Studio" logo was crafted to symbolize swift creativity and timeless design. We focused on clean geometric shapes to ensure the brand stands out in both digital and print media.

Design Approach

Using a monochromatic palette with the signature #9dae11 accent, we created a visual language that is both professional and energetic. The typography is modern sans-serif to reflect forward-thinking innovation.
