Why These Fonts?

ဒီ Font တွေက Logo Design, Brand Identity, Display Typography တွေမှာအသုံးပြုဖို့ သီးသန့် Design လုပ်ထားတဲ့ Professional Typefaces တွေဖြစ်ပါတယ်။ Strong Brand Presence ဖန်တီးနိုင်ပြီး Versatile ဖြစ်ပါတယ်။

Featured Logo Fonts

Gendy

Otf ttf Uppercase Lowercase Multilingual Alternate Ligature

Miles

Miles Font is a Elegant Script font. It's perfect for branding, wedding invitations, photography, Neon Sign, bloggers, logos, greeting cards, Logo, Quotes and more.

Noveau

Noveau is a luxury uppercase typeface designed for prestige, iconic characters, and timeless elegance. Every letter is crafted with refined detail, making it the perfect choice for premium logos, fashion branding, and high-end editorial design.

Recline

Recline comes in a modern style featuring 10 weights, 5 straight & angled, making it easy to adapt to your design. An elegant typeface, Recline is perfect for corporate identities, websites, publishing, titles, books, magazines, business cards, logos, product labels, packaging, or any advertising purpose.

Remodia

Remodia is a experimental sans font with fun alternate combination.. Remodia come with alternate playful look and flownes curve make it more attractive, you have the flexibility to mix & match to create the perfect combination.

Royal

Recommended using Adobe Illustrator or Adobe Photoshop.

Sink

otf ttf Uppercase Lowercase Multilingual Alternate Ligature

Tips for Using Logo Fonts

  • • Font တစ်ခုကို Logo မှာသုံးတဲ့အခါ Licensing ကိုသေချာစစ်ဆေးပါ
  • • Different weights နဲ့ styles တွေကို experiment လုပ်ကြည့်ပါ
  • • Letter spacing နဲ့ kerning ကို adjust လုပ်ပြီး unique look ဖန်တီးပါ
  • • Brand personality နဲ့ကိုက်ညီမှုကိုအရင် prioritize လုပ်ပါ

Note: Typefaces တွေကို Adobe Illustrator မှာ Create Outlines လုပ်ပြီး လိုအပ်သလိုကစားနိုင်ပါတယ်။
